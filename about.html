WHO I AM

LUCKYFROG

JOSE LOPEZ

I was born in 1995, to two immigrants; from Spain and Mexico. I am a middle child of the last three, and have two cats, and one dog. I create music, as well as do a lot of working with my hands from building cars, carving logs, or making furniture, to delicately assembling batteries, and electronics.

02

ESSENCE

  • DESIGN

    I draw a lot of my creativity from viewing other cultures, both classical and modern. Having heritage in both Spain and Mexico bring a Romantic touch to elements I try to incorporate into an aesthetic. I believe to make something both unique and memorable, it has to draw on something that is fundamental to the human experience.

  • TECHNOLOGY

    Building a new world of tomorrow. Leaving nothing to chance I look to make a difference in the world of technology. Through making people see technology differently, and interact more consciously; we can shape how the world works, and redefine what is possible.

  • SPIRIT

    Everything with essence has a sort of spirit, that drives and makes it tick. It turns an ordinary car into a lifelong friend, that special gift from someone to spring memories, and gives monuments stories to tell. Recognizing the spirit, brings a sense of direction and purpose to an otherwise ordinary product, website, or service.
